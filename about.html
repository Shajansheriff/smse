About us

College of Engineering, Guindy

College of Engineering, Guindy(CEG) is one of the earliest engineering institutions to have been established in India, with a 225 year legacy of empowerment through knowledge. The college provides various avenues for feeding the inquisitive young minds and to channel their passion towards achieving zenith’s height. The motto “Labor Omnia Vincit” (work conquers all) instills the fundamental sense of hardwork and discipline to accomplish their goals.

Mech

Department of Mechanical Engineering

The Department of Mechanical Engineering , one of the oldest departments in the college, offers two undergraduate programs, Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science and Engineering. The Central Workshop present in the department, holds many sophisticated machines and tools for the ease of students to learn from.

SMSE

Society of Materials Science Engineers (SMSE) is a student run body, formed exclusively for materials science enthusiasts under the B.E. Materials Science program. This community constantly strives for excellence by illuminating the young minds and building technical knowledge, soft skills and personality traits of the students, thereby ensuring holistic development of the budding materials science engineers. The society also helps students keep in touch with current trends in Materials Science by organizing timely seminars, workshops and guest lectures. Every year, SMSE conducts two major symposiums, Krysta (intra college symposium) and Quanta(inter college symposium).
