SYMPOSIA

The Society of Materials Science and Engineers conducts two mega symposia namely Krysta and Quanta which are the highlights of the year calendar!

Krysta

Krysta is a well-renowned intra-college symposium, with its first edition in 2011, aimed at encouraging the students to strengthen inter-department ties. It also helps enhance technical skills by collaborating with industries and research institutions through workshops. Every year, there are a few signature events conducted along with newly added on-spot and online events which bring out a unique mix of intelligence, insight and entertainment.

Quanta

Quanta is a national level techno-management fest, conducted each year, to bring together, students and professionals from across the country to showcase their proficiency and exchange novel ideas regarding various aspects of Materials Science.
With the opening version conducted in the year 2010, it provides budding engineers, various opportunities to explore the recent advances in field of the materials science and a platform to bridge the gap between the students and industry professionals & technocrats. With a plethora of signature technical and non-technical events, it is the perfect ensemble for both geeks and fun lovers to indulge in.
