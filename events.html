Career Guidance Program


First Phase:

The academic year 2018-2019, was kick-started with a mega event on Career Guidance, divided into different phases. The first phase of the Career Guidance Program was aimed at motivating the young minds to explore the career opportunities in Materials Science and Engineering. We had Dr.T.Thyagarajan, Director of CUIC, presiding over the session and illuminating the young minds bubbling with passion for the field of Materials Science, encouraging them to stay on par with their dreams. We witnessed an overwhelming response from about 150 members of the audience, making it a grand success.



Second Phase:

The second phase of the Career Guidance Program was conducted in Tag Audi on September 5, 2018, on the occasion of Teacher's day. The program was conducted with the objective of the creating awareness among UPSC aspirants and Army, Navy cadets, about the career opportunities in the Civil Services and the Indian Armed Forces. The notable speakers, who presided over the session were Mr. Nanda Kumar IRS, an dyslexic school dropout who excelled in UPSC exams, Lieutenant Esan, a training offer at the Officers Training Academy and Miss. Annapoorani, the first female military officer from Theni district. The event witnessed a humongous crowd of about 500 highly driven and motivated young minds, striving for excellence with an aching passion for serving the nation.
